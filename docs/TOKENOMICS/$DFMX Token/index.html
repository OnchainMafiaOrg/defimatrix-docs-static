🪙 $DFMX Token

Tokenomics - The DeFiMatrix Economy Enhanced 🌍

The core of our ecosystem is underpinned by the deliberate limitation of the total supply to 2 billion DFMX tokens, a strategic approach to ensure scarcity and maintain the token's value. The allocation has been meticulously structured to support various facets of our ecosystem and secure its long-term success:

With a total token supply of 50,000,000 DFMX, the allocations are shown as follows:

  • Liquidity Bootstrapping Pool (LBP) (15% - 7,500,000 DFMX): Fully available at the Token Generation Event (TGE), this allocation ensures immediate liquidity and market participation, facilitating the platform's dynamic entry into the market.

  • Development (15% - 7,500,000 DFMX): With 50% accessible at TGE and the remainder vested over 6 months, this allocation is critical for funding continuous innovation and platform enhancements, keeping DeFiMatrix at the leading edge of technology.

  • Liquidity Pool (15% - 7,500,000 DFMX): Committed to ensuring seamless transactions and sustained liquidity across the platform, this allocation underpins an efficient DeFi ecosystem, with no initial unlock at TGE to maintain market stability.

  • Marketing (10% - 5,000,000 DFMX): 30% available at TGE with the rest vested over 4 months, aimed at broadening DeFiMatrix's reach and reinforcing our presence in the market through strategic marketing efforts.

  • Key Opinion Leaders (KOL) Round (5% - 2,500,000 DFMX): With no initial unlock at TGE and a total vesting period of 15 months, this allocation is designed to engage influencers and advocates in promoting DeFiMatrix, leveraging their networks for platform growth.

  • Partnerships (5% - 2,500,000 DFMX): Also with no unlock at TGE and vested over 15 months, dedicated to forging strategic collaborations that enhance the DeFiMatrix ecosystem and drive mutual growth.

  • Advisors (5% - 2,500,000 DFMX): Without any tokens unlocked at TGE and a 15-month vesting schedule, this allocation compensates advisors who contribute valuable insights and strategies for DeFiMatrix's development.

  • Team & Founders (15% - 7,500,000 DFMX): Demonstrating a commitment to the platform's long-term success, this allocation has no initial unlock and is vested over 30 months, aligning the team's and founders' interests with the platform's goals.

  • Ecosystem Rewards (10% - 5,000,000 DFMX): Allocated for rewarding user participation and engagement within the DeFiMatrix ecosystem, this segment has no initial unlock and a 36-month vesting period to sustain community involvement.

  • Reserve Fund (5% - 2,500,000 DFMX): Designed to provide financial flexibility and cater to unforeseen needs, with no unlock at TGE and a 24-month vesting period, ensuring the platform's resilience and adaptability.
